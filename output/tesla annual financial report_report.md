### 3.1 Market Risks  
- Intensifying competition from legacy automakers and new EV entrants.  
- Volatile raw‑material prices (lithium, nickel).  
- Shifts in government EV incentives or emissions regulations.  
- Global macro‑economic slowdown reducing discretionary spending.  

### 3.2 Operational Risks  
- Potential production bottlenecks or supply‑chain disruptions.  
- Rapid battery‑technology advances by rivals.  
- Product recalls or safety incidents (e.g., Autopilot concerns).  
- Labor disputes at key facilities.  

### 3.3 Financial Risks  
- Existing debt levels, though moderate, could become problematic if cash flow deteriorates.  
- Currency exposure due to international sales and supply chains.  

> **Call‑out (Urgent):** Supply‑chain fragility and competitive pressure are the top‑priority risks requiring immediate mitigation actions.
